BASKETBALL


ANNOUNCEMENT!!

Registration for Inter-Municipality BASKETBALL Tournament is now open for 17 years old and below. Take note that WITHOUT THE CONFIRMATION from the SK Chairpersons of your certain barangay within Sorsogon City (EAST, WEST and BACON DISTRICTS), entry will be invalid. If you are interested to join, kindly contact your SK Chairperson for registration of entry and validation.

Further, West District Tryout for 17 years old and below will start on AUGUST 29, 2022 (Monday), 1pm at PANGPANG GYMNASIUM. Look for Coach Johnny Butac. Meanwhile, Schedule of TRYOUTS for EAST AND BACON are to be announced soon. WAIT for our NEXT ANNOUNCEMENT.

NOTE: SK CITY FEDERATION WILL FOCUS ONLY ON 17 YEARS AND BELOW CATEGORY WHILE OPEN AGE CATEGORY will be validated by the LGU-Sorsogon City.


FOR REGISTRATION:
CONTACT : 09090909090
EMAIL : sorsogonbasketballball@gmail.com

LOCATION

  • Corner Imelda Roces Avenue,
  • Barangay 38, Gogon,
  • Legazpi City, Albay, Philippines 4500
