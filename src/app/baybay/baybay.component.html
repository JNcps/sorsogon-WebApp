Sorsogon Tourist Spot


Halabang Baybay Beach

Photo courtesy

Halabang Baybay is a long stretch of fine golden brown colored sand or in our native dialect “Halabang Baybay“. Its picturesque view is a perfect venue for photoshoots. The water is clear. However, there are rocks and other rock formations along the shoreline. You have to walk over those rocks in order to swim. This place is a few meters away from Padaraw Beach.



Halabang Baybay, Caricaran, Bacon District, Sorsogon City
