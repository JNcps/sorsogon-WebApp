    Set in Legazpi, 6.7 km from Cagsawa Ruins, Emerald Boutique Hotel offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a shared lounge and a garden.     Popular points of interest near Emerald Boutique Hotel include Ibalong Centrum for Recreation, Bariw and Legazpi. The nearest airport is Naga Airport, 83 km from the hotel.
