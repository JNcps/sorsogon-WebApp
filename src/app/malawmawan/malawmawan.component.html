Malawmawan Island Malawmawan Island is an underdeveloped, isolated island off the port of Macalaya town. The island features long stretches of beaches, a beautiful but slightly polluted sandbar, and a secluded lagoon. The island was originally open to overnight stays, but it is currently inhabited by a family claiming ownership of the land. With its lack of amenities and existing ownership disputes, guests are advised to visit the island at their own risk.
