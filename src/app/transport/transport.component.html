OVERVIEW

Philippine Airlines

Introduction

Philippine Airlines (PR) is a full flight airline operating domestic & international routes. The airline gives the passenger with a cabin baggage allowance of 7 kg and maximum size of 45 inch (L + W + H). The passengers flying international route with Philippine Airlines will get free checked baggage allowance of 30 kg. Passengers can check in at the airport 180 minutes prior to the departure time. Check in online is possible for flights with the airline on Philippine Airlines official website. It can be processed 1 days prior to flight departure time.

ABOUT

Philippine Airlines

Company Profile

Philippine Airlines which operates with IATA code (PR) and ICAO code PAL, is a full flight airline that flies domestic & international routes. The airline flies to destinations all over the world with its main hub located in Ninoy Aquino International Airport (MNL).

History

Philippine Airlines operating with IATA code (PR) and ICAO code (PAL) runs under the management of PAL Holdings, Inc.. In 1941, this airline was established. Throughout the years, Philippine Airlines continued to flourish its business by maintaining a worthwhile and pleasant experience for their passengers. This airline was first flown on 15-03-1941. Philippine Airlines has been awarded Skytrax Award in 2018.

Flight Attendant

Philippine Airlines cabin crew wears a uniform in black and white. For male flight attendants, they wear white shirt, black suit, tie and black trousers. For female flight attendants, they wear white shirt, black suit, scarf and black skirt. The uniform itself has been beautifully laid out, making the crew look more refined.

FACILITIES AND SERVICES

Philippine Airlines

Cabin Baggage

Passengers of Philippine Airlines are given a cabin baggage allowance included in their ticket fare. The max weight of the baggage is 7 kg, while the max size of the luggage put in the cabin is 45 inch (L + W + H).

Checked Baggage

Free checked baggage allowance is given to the passengers flying on Philippine Airlines. Passengers flying domestic route do not get checked baggage allowance, but passengers on international flights will get 30 kg free checked baggage allowance.

Excess Baggage

Excess checked baggage allowance weight can be purchased on Airpaz.com. Disclaimer: Information stated above is intended for general guide only, and may be changed by the airline with no prior notification. Contact the airline for further information.

Seat
